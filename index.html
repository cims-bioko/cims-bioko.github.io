CIMS Bioko

What is CIMS?

Short for Campaign Information Management System, it is a system for facilitating outreach campaigns using location-sensitive mobile data collection.

Who made this?

The CIMS was developed by a number of contributors at the University of Southern Maine under the direction of Computer Science professor Bruce MacLeod and in collaboration with MCDI.

What does it do?

It enables MCDI's workers on Bioko to collect data using mobile computing devices with limited connectivity. It organizes forms and facilitates data entry by pre-filling information based on location.

Can I use it?

Yes. CIMS is composed of a number of freely-available software packages, many of which are open-source. At a minimum you need an Android-based tablet and a networked computer.
